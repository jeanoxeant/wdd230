Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Site Name

Santo Domingo Chamber of Commerce

Site Purpose

The Dominican Chamber of Commerce is a nonprofit trade association representing the interest of independent businesses which owners are Dominican Republic’s nationals or their descendants. We provide outreach services to corporations that want to capture the booming Hispanic market by providing counseling on marketing strategies to reach Hispanic retailers and consumers. We advocate for pro-business policies that help incentive the insertion of minorities into entrepreneurship.
